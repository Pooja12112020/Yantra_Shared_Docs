At Yantra Dev App, we respect your privacy and give you full control over your data.

How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your account and associated data, please send an email to:

info@innovapoint.com

Use the subject line: "Delete My Account"

What will be deleted?

  • Your account and login credentials
  • Any user preferences and personal information (if stored)

What may be retained?

  • Any data required to comply with legal obligations (e.g., billing records)

We will process your request within 7 business days.
